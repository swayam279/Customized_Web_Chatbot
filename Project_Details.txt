Website Chatbot Generator

Problem Statement: Businesses and users often need quick answers from a specific website but are forced to manually browse through pages. There is no easy way to instantly turn any website into an interactive assistant without building custom solutions from scratch.

Expected Outcomes:

1) Given any valid URL, a working chatbot is ready to use within moments
2) Chatbot answers are strictly based on the website's content only
3) If the answer is not found on the site, the bot clearly says so instead of hallucinating
4) Follow-up questions work naturally within the same conversation
